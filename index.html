About App4brides

Your Wedding plans are categorized under different activities or item. Click on the applicable activity or item. The buttons before or after an activity or item indicates there are sub categories under. You can write, edit, delete and store notes in your device by clicking on the sub activity or item in blue.

Welcome to APP4BRIDES. Congratulations!!!!!!! This is probably the most exciting time of your life!!!!!!!! And we at Feminineedge thought it shouldn't be the most stressfull too. So we developed this app to help you let off steam and have fun while planning your big day. APP4BRIDE would help you keep tab on your plans.The App contains various checklists to help you with planning and a note section where you can type your notes, edit, delete and save notes on your phone, tablets or desktop. Also visit App4brides Mall for the lastest on bridal couture, accessories and get the chance to get designer wedding gowns at wow prices. App4brides is user friendly, users only have to click on the button on each tab. Start by clicking on checklist panel below or open up the note section also below to start.
